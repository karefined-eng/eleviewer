Welcome to EleViewer 🐘

Every file your professor throws at you. One free app.

EleViewer is your lightweight study workspace. It opens and edits DOCX, XLSX, PDF, Markdown, and TXT — all in one place. Everything a student needs to read, write, and organize coursework — and nothing that slows an old laptop down. No telemetry, no forced cloud accounts, and no loading screens.

Survival Shortcuts ⚡

Master these three shortcuts to fly through your workflow:

Alt+V : Toggle Vault Sidebar
Ctrl+Q : Quick Switcher (fuzzy find files)
Ctrl+T : Split View Web Browser

Safety Net Active 🛡️

We've got your back. Draft Auto-Save silently backs up your unsaved text every 60 seconds so you never lose a fleeting thought to a crash or an accidental 'Don't Save'.

What's New in v1.3.0?
